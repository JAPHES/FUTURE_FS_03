About SalonConnect

Salon beauty services made easier, safer, and closer to home.

SalonConnect is a simple booking platform that connects customers with trusted stylists for at-home beauty services. Whether you need hair styling, makeup, nail care, or skincare, the platform helps you discover professionals, compare options, and schedule appointments with ease.

We also give stylists a place to showcase their skills, manage their availability, and reach more customers in a clean and beginner-friendly digital space. The goal is to make salon services more convenient for clients and more visible for providers.
